Sarah Lewin: Science Writer

I am a science writer, the current web intern at Science Friday, and a final-semester graduate student at New York University's Science, Health and Environmental Reporting program. I have written for IEEE Spectrum magazine and blogs, The Scientist, WGBH's Inside NOVA, Boston Children's Hospital's science and innovation blog, and Johns Hopkins Medicine Magazine as well as on Scienceline.org, where I am the current multimedia editor. I've reported and written blogs, put together interactive data visualizations, promoted organizations with social media, copyedited, led an improv comedy group, and fit complicated data into simple press releases. Super shiny resume here, and my twitter feed is here. Email me here.

If you're looking for my blog, that's back on Wordpress. Check out my (admittedly not recently updated) posts explaining everything from the Tetris Effect to How Not to Sit in a Chair.
